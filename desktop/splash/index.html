Citadel service isn't responding

Citadel runs as a Windows service in the background. It looks like the service isn't up right now.

Try this, in order:

  1. Wait a few more seconds and click Retry below — services sometimes take 10–20 seconds to start.
  2. Restart your PC. Citadel will auto-start at boot.
  3. Still stuck? Email support@citadels.cc with the log file from C:\Citadel\data\service.log.

Advanced: open services.msc, find Citadel DayZ Server Controller, right-click → Start.
